About Granite Bay AI

Granite Bay AI was founded with a simple mission: to help local business owners compete and win in their markets using cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Our founder, Dan Downey, recognized that small to medium-sized businesses were losing countless leads and opportunities because they lacked the resources for 24/7 customer engagement. After years of working with local businesses in the Granite Bay area and beyond, we developed an AI chatbot platform specifically designed for the unique needs of local service providers, real estate professionals, healthcare practices, and e-commerce businesses.
